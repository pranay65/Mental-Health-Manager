Top Mental Health Resources

  • MentalHealth.gov

    A comprehensive guide to mental health topics, including depression, anxiety, and substance use. You can also access information about treatments and recovery options.

  • World Health Organization: Mental Health

    The WHO provides global information on mental health conditions and strategies for promoting mental well-being. This is a valuable resource for international perspectives and policies.

  • National Institute of Mental Health

    Find up-to-date research, resources, and information on a variety of mental health conditions, from anxiety disorders to PTSD. The NIMH also offers educational resources and clinical trial information.

  • Headspace

    Headspace is a mindfulness and meditation app that offers guided meditation to reduce stress and improve focus. It's a great way to incorporate mindfulness into your daily routine.

  • 7 Cups

    Offering free emotional support and counseling, 7 Cups connects users with trained listeners and licensed therapists, providing an outlet for those who may feel isolated.

  • Crisis Text Line

    Crisis Text Line offers free, 24/7 support for those in crisis. Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a crisis counselor. They provide confidential support for any type of mental health emergency.

  • Samaritans

    A UK-based charity, Samaritans offers a 24/7 helpline for anyone in distress. They provide free support through their hotline and email services for those struggling with emotional pain.
